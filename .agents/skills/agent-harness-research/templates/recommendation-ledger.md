Quality rules:

- One recommendation is one non-duplicated mechanism, not a rewording.
- Fifty is the maximum for a deep run, never a quota.
- Runtime hooks, routing, permissions, concurrency, model choice, memory promotion, and global gates remain `todo` until the owner approves.
- Group approved candidates into coherent implementation cards only after quality and governance reviews exist.
